Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of coaching style does Coach Lili have?

My coaching style is rooted in emotional safety, cultural awareness, and identity reclamation.

Sessions are flexible but intentional, blending supportive conversation, reflective questions, boundary work, and intuitive insight.

How are coaching sessions structured?

Redemption Era coaching sessions are 60 minutes in length and held virtually.

This space allows for honest expression, emotional release, and real-time reflection.

You are not expected to have it all together; you are simply invited to show up as you are.

How do you handle communication between coaching sessions?

We use services like Google Meet or Zoom for coaching sessions, but can be reached between sessions through Discord, Instagram, or Telegram.

Clients with active packages may reach out via instant messaging, voice messaging, or email.

Please allow 48 hours for a response (Monday-Friday only).

Coaching is not an emergency or crisis support service.

What payment methods are accepted?

All packages must be paid in full via PayPal before the first session.

Once payment is received we will confirm your schedule and send session details.

Do you offer payment arrangements or sliding scale pricing?

Payment arrangements and sliding scale options are available for select clients.

To inquire, please email us with a brief note describing which package you are interested in and your situation.

We will respond with available options and instructions for requesting an invoice.

What's your policy on cancelling or rescheduling coaching sessions?

A minimum of 24-hour notice is required to cancel or reschedule a session.

One grace reschedule is allowed per package, based on availability.

What's your policy on showing up to coaching sessions late?

Sessions begin and end on time.

If you are more than 15 minutes late without notice, the session may be forfeited and marked as a no-show.

No refunds will be provided for missed or late sessions.

Do packages expire after a certain period of time?

Yes, each package must be used within its stated validity window.

Sessions not used within that window will expire and cannot be redeemed after the expiration date.

Do you offer refunds?

No, we do not offer refunds once sessions begin.

Please be sure of your booking before placing a reservation.

If you have an exceptional circumstance, contact us directly and we will review the request on a case by case basis.
